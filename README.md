# 🛒 Shri Jain Stationery Mart – E-Commerce Web Application

📍 **Shri Jain Stationery Mart** is a modern, responsive e-commerce web application developed to digitize the operations of a traditional stationery store located at **Arya Samaj Road, Kota, Rajasthan**. The platform enables customers to browse products, manage carts, place orders, complete secure payments, and track deliveries through a centralized digital system.

🌐 **Live Demo:** https://shri-jain-stationery-mart.onrender.com

🎯 **Project Objective**

Transform an offline retail business into a fully digital storefront, improving customer convenience, automating order processing, reducing manual work, and enhancing overall business efficiency through a scalable web-based solution.

✨ **Customer Features**

✅ Interactive landing page with store details and featured products
✅ Categorized stationery product catalog
🔍 Product search and filtering functionality
🛒 Add-to-Cart and Buy-Now options
📦 Cart management with quantity updates and total calculation
🏠 Secure checkout with delivery address management
💳 Payment options: UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking (Sandbox)
📧 Automated order confirmation with unique Order ID
👤 User Registration & Login System
📊 Customer Dashboard with order history and tracking
📱 Fully responsive and mobile-friendly design

🛠️ **Admin Portal & Management Dashboard**

🖥️ Comprehensive Admin Dashboard for complete store management
📦 Product Management (Add, Edit, Delete, Update Products)
🏷️ Category & Brand Management
📊 Inventory Management with real-time stock monitoring
⚠️ Low Stock Alerts & Inventory Notifications
🎟️ Coupon & Discount Management System
📋 Order Management Dashboard for processing customer orders
💰 Revenue Dashboard with sales analytics and business insights
📈 Product Performance & Usage Analytics
👥 Customer Management and Order Monitoring
🔐 Audit Logs for tracking admin activities and system changes
🔄 Automatic inventory updates after successful purchases
⚡ Easy product onboarding through the admin panel

🤖 **Automation Workflow**

🔄 Order details are automatically stored in Google Sheets via Webhooks
📧 Instant order confirmation emails sent to customers
📩 Admin receives automated notifications for new orders
🚚 Delivery status updates sent through automated email workflows
✅ Delivery completion confirmation sent automatically to customers
📦 Inventory levels updated automatically after each purchase

💡 **Key Capabilities**

🚀 Smooth product browsing and checkout experience
📱 Mobile-first responsive design
⚡ Automated order and inventory management
📊 Business insights through analytics dashboards
🔐 Secure user authentication and transaction flow
⏳ Reduced manual administrative workload

🧠 **Technologies Used**

🎨 Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
⚙️ Backend: Node.js / PHP
🗄️ Database: MySQL / MongoDB
📊 SaaS Admin Dashboard & Analytics
📈 Chart.js for revenue, sales, and inventory visualizations
🔗 Automation: Webhooks, Google Sheets, Email Automation
💳 Payment Gateway Integration (Sandbox Environment)

🏆 **Outcome**

Successfully transformed a traditional stationery store into a scalable digital commerce platform, providing a seamless shopping experience for customers while empowering administrators with advanced tools for inventory control, sales monitoring, revenue tracking, coupon management, audit logging, and complete business operations through a centralized dashboard.
